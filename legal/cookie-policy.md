## 1. What Are Cookies?

1.1. **Cookies** are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website or use an app. They help the Platform function, remember your preferences, and understand how the Platform is used.

1.2. We also use **similar technologies** such as local storage, software development kits (SDKs), pixels, and device identifiers. In this Policy, we refer to all of these collectively as "**cookies**".

1.3. Cookies may be:
   - **First-party** — set by QuickBite; or
   - **Third-party** — set by our service providers (e.g., analytics or payment partners).

They may also be **session** cookies (deleted when you close the app/browser) or **persistent** cookies (remaining until they expire or you delete them).

---

## 2. Categories of Cookies We Use

2.1. **Strictly necessary cookies.** Required for the Platform to function — for example, to keep you signed in, maintain your session and cart, route requests securely, and prevent fraud. These cannot be switched off through our consent tools.

2.2. **Functional cookies.** Remember your choices and preferences (such as saved addresses, language, or location settings) to provide a more personalised experience.

2.3. **Analytics / performance cookies.** Help us understand how users interact with the Platform (e.g., pages visited, features used, errors encountered) so we can improve it. These are used **only with your consent** where required.

2.4. **Marketing / advertising cookies.** Used, **only with your consent**, to measure campaigns and show you relevant promotions on and off the Platform.
